## NOTES ON TEAM STRUCTURE

- Teams 1, 2, and 3 operate **all 7 days** (weekdays and weekends).
- Teams 4 and 5 operate **weekdays only** — they do not exist on nights or weekends.
- On weekends, the attending-to-resident ratio may be as low as 1 attending + 1 resident. Max team size is 1 attending + 1 senior + 2 juniors = 4 people.
- Senior residents are scheduled **independently** from junior residents but work alongside them on shift. Do not schedule juniors and seniors as a coupled unit — they have separate assignment processes.
