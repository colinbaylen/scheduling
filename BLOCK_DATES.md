# Block Dates — Academic Year 2025–2026

Each block is a 2-week period. Blocks are labeled by number (1–13) and half (A = first two weeks, B = second two weeks). All blocks run Monday–Sunday except Block 1A, which begins on Tuesday July 1 (the academic year start date).

## Teaching Shift Window

Blocks **1A through 5B** (Jul 1 – Nov 2, 2025) fall within the teaching months of July, August, September, and October. During these blocks, senior residents must be assigned to teaching shifts (DT at Bellevue, TT at Tisch) aligned with the medical student rotation schedule provided by UME faculty.

## Notes on Block Naming

- Each numbered period (1–13) contains two consecutive 2-week blocks, labeled **A** and **B**.
- A-blocks always start on Monday (except 1A).
- B-blocks always start on Monday, 14 days after the corresponding A-block.
- The EM block schedule runs **Monday–Sunday**, but the academic duty-hour week runs **Sunday 00:00–Saturday 23:59**. Sunday hours worked at the end of one block count toward the following academic week.
